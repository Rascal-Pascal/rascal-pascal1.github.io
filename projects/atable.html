A Table Collection

Fabrica for Atipico

2015

Italian ceramic / beech wood / stainless steel

A tableware collection made in collaboratIon with Fabrica designers, a work founded on the different cultural backgrounds and eating traditions.

The table nowadays is a melting pot of different cultures, customs and traditions. The contemporary palate seeks increasing involvement in multiethnic culinary experiences and the dining table is the physical space for meetings and exchanges. This is the driving concept behind ‘A Table’, a new collection of objects and accessories for the table – plates, bowls, glasses, baskets, carafes, cutting boards, cutlery and linen – designed by Fabrica for Atipico.
