Robin Copper

2013

lamp

copper + wood bended sandwich profile

An asymmetric profile is combined with freely attachable lightclips. Robin Copper is totally flexible in its positioning. It sets lightspots and does not become a lamp specified on one use. Depending on the arrangement of the clips the position of the profile it is a desk lamp, floor lamp or pendant lamp and gives the user the opportunity to playfully reinvent personal light situations.
