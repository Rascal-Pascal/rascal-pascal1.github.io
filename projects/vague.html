Vague

2014

side table / coffee table

wood, coated aluminum, glass

The vague table takes its inspiration in wave sheets normally seen in architecture on roofs or factory buildings. By transferring it into a table, the waves not only provide maximum stability on minim material but also form a place for presentation. The glass plate on top, almost like a magnifying glass, makes every object presented underneath become precious. It is a display for objects that have a story. Giving focus to your memories or space to place magazines. If preferred you can flip the table around to have a flat wooden surface. Space inside the tubes can be used for storage. The direct transition between the wave and the wooden tube creates a fresh new look, making this table both functional and elegant.
